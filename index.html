General Safety Features

  • Wear your seatbelt at all times while seated.
  • Follow the safety instructions from the flight attendants.
  • Keep your electronic devices switched off during take-off and landing.
  • Know the location of emergency exits and life vests.

Airport Rules & Regulations

  • Do not bring prohibited items through security checks (e.g., sharp objects, liquids over 100ml).
  • Arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before your international flight and 1 hour before domestic flights.
  • Follow the signs and directions from airport personnel for security checks and boarding procedures.

Ways to Dispose of Waste at the Airport

  • Use the designated waste bins for recycling (paper, plastic, glass).
  • Dispose of any electronic waste in special bins for batteries and old electronics.
  • Food waste should be placed in the proper bins to maintain cleanliness.
