THE KENYA AIRWAYS

Kenya Airways, founded in 1977, is the flag carrier of Kenya. The airline has grown to become a prominent operator in East Africa, providing both domestic and international services. Its hub is located in Nairobi at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. The airline serves numerous destinations across Africa, Europe, and the Middle East.
